LEO LOPE LOFRANCO

A Research Scientist.
A Tech-entrepreneur.
An Avid Traveler.

Leo Lope is a pre-med student at the Ateneo de Manila University. He is passionate about science and technology. His lifelong dream is to become a successful entrepreneur who relies on hard data and thorough scientific research.

He worked as a research assistant at the University of Genoa in Italy and at Harvard in Cambridge Massachussetts. To live up to his dreams, he co-founded a tech startup called Muber, an innovative peer-to-peer package delivery system and marketplace. Most of his time is dedicated in working on his startup.

Outside of school, he enjoys traveling, coding, and volunteering. He has traveled across the U.S., Europe, and Asia. He is an avid fan of collaborative consumption and very excited about the future of technology. Every week, he also volunteers in a school of disabled children to teach kids basic math and science.

He lives in the Manila, Philippines, but used to live in Genoa and Cambridge.
